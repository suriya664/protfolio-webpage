InsightFlow — Enterprise analytics operating system

A unified intelligence platform helping operations leaders make faster decisions with cross-team visibility.

Visit product

Overview

InsightFlow consolidates disjointed analytics tools into a single, customizable workspace. I was brought in as the product design lead to define the product vision, validate the core workflows, and build a design system that could scale with rapid iteration and enterprise demands.

Problem

Operations teams relied on static dashboards that couldn’t keep pace with their decisions. Data lived in siloed tools, leading to duplicated work and misalignment across departments.

Outcome

  • Shipped MVP in 4 months with 97% onboarding completion.
  • Reduced average insight discovery time from 2 days to 40 minutes.
  • Built design system with 180+ documented components adopted by engineering.

Process highlights

  1. Ran discovery interviews with 24 stakeholders across 6 departments.
  2. Mapped end-to-end journeys and defined success metrics tied to OKRs.
  3. Rapidly prototyped dashboard builder concepts to validate mental models.
  4. Codified design tokens and component library with accessibility baked in.
